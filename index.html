Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 8, 2025

Freestyle Cards ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

  • Contact Info: Email Address (for account verification, purchase confirmation, and support).
  • User Content: Gameplay content, such as game settings, custom decks, personalized game table designs and language preference.
  • Purchases: Purchase history and subscription status to provide app functionality and verify in-app purchases.

How We Use Your Data

  • Verify purchases and subscriptions through Apple and RevenueCat.
  • Save your custom game settings and personalization in Firebase.
  • Provide support if you contact us via our support form.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share it with service providers who help us operate the app (e.g., Firebase, RevenueCat).

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, but no method is 100% secure.

Your Choices

You can contact us through our support form to request deletion of your data.

Changes

We may update this policy occasionally. We recommend reviewing it periodically.
